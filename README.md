# Supply Chain KPI Tracker

## Project Overview
End-to-end supply chain analytics project analyzing 2 years of operational data
across 15 suppliers, 6 warehouses, and 15 major customers in Saudi Arabia and the Middle East.

## KPIs Tracked
- On-Time Delivery Rate
- Order Fill Rate
- Perfect Order Rate
- Inventory Turnover
- Supplier Defect Rate
- Freight Cost per Unit
- Warehouse Utilization
- Customer Satisfaction Score
- Return Rate
- Stockout Rate

## Tools & Technologies
- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- **SQL** (SQLite queries for data analysis)
- **Power BI** (Interactive dashboard)
- **Excel** (Report generation)

## Dataset
- 10 interconnected CSV files
- 8,000+ records covering Jan 2023 - Dec 2024
- Realistic supply chain data for the Middle East region

## Key Findings
1. On-Time Delivery Rate needs improvement with specific suppliers
2. Perfect Order Rate can be increased by addressing quality issues
3. Warehouse utilization varies significantly across locations
4. Customer satisfaction correlates strongly with delivery speed
